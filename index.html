Expert Testimony and Litigation Support

    Serving leading law firms and clients throughout Asia, JTD provides dedicated expert witness services at very affordable rates or capped "all-in" packages. The suite of support services includes free initial consultations, preliminary and expert opinions, referrals to outside experts, settlement discussions, resolution talks between case experts and court testimony. JTD has experience with both the Supreme Court of Singapore and the Singapore International Arbitration Centre (SIAC). Legal disputes happen when parties are unable to resolve their differences and are forced to seek legal or administrative remedies. Although protracted litigation may provide a steady source of consulting revenue to lawyers and consultants, an accomplished, knowledgeable expert witness who helps drive resolutions and settlements delivers considerable savings and value to its clients.

Risk Management

    When it comes to protecting your exposure, there are "no silver bullets" or "one size fits all" solutions. Beware of jargon, complex models and "structured solutions". Your hedging costs generate revenue for the risk providers. JTD provides a complete focus on your firm's specific exposures and rigorously evaluates what may best suit your firm’s risk profile, culture, and resources. JTD engages with its clients to jointly devise the most optimal,    cost-effective risk strategies. JTD does not trade energy stocks or derivatives or scrupulously avoids any potential conflicts of interest.

Singapore Bunker Market

The Client:  Independent Asian refiner sought to launch bunker trading operations in 2013 which would require the firm to secure long-term storage leases at an independent terminal, hire staff, arrange barges and build up client's base in the world's largest (and most competitive) marine fuels market.

Work:  Delivered comprehensive assessment of capital required, staffing, risks and returns for Client and multiple scenarios for market participation including non-entry.

Outcome:  After reviewing findings from JTD, the Client decided to defer its entry into the Singapore bunker market in 2013. JTD's services to the Client ended.

Epilogue: In late 2014, the Singapore marine fuels the market suffered a major blow with the insolvency of the world's 7th largest bunker supplier, OW Bunker. The Client dodged a huge bullet!

Risk Management

The Client: A small, independent Asian refinery recently spun off by a large integrated energy firm needed to set up a risk management program to monitor and hedge their refining margins.

Background: The Client had no previous experience with hedging and derivatives as their former owner had performed these functions on their behalf. The Client requested a quote for an intensive, customized 2 full day course to explain the basics of risk management and develop recommended strategies for hedging their refinery margins. Under their Non-Disclosure Agreements, the Client provided JTD with proprietary documents on the purchase and supply agreements and refinery operations.

Action:  Within 2 weeks from the initial contact from the Client, JTD delivered a complete 2-day training package for their entire staff from the Chief Executive Officer to the operations support staff. In response to the Client's request on a longer-dated strategy to hedge their refinery margin risk from early 2017 and beyond, JTD recommended that the Client aggressively lock in forwarding time spreads in anticipation of market structure shifting into steeper backwardation. This strategy proved to be sound.

Epilogue:  The Client was pleased with the course and eventually recruited an experienced risk manager to implement the policies.
